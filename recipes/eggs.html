Yotam Ottolenghi's Baked Eggs with Yogurt and Chile

Baked eggs topped with yogurt and chile!

This is the perfect Turkish inspired brunch.

This is the perfect dish for two!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb arugula
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • Pinch of chile flakes
  • 6 sage leaves, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300Â°F.
  2. Place arugula, oil, and some salt in large pan. Saute on medium for 5 minutes.
  3. Transfer to ovenproof dish. Make four deep indentations in the arugula.
  4. Place eggs into indentations. Bake eggs and arugula for 10 to 15 minutes, until whites are set.
  5. Mix yogurt with garlic and salt.
  6. Melt butter in small pan. Add the sweet paprika and chile flakes and fry for 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Add the sage to the butter for a few seconds.
  8. Remove eggs from oven. Spoon yogurt over the arugla and pour hot chile butter over the arugla and eggs.
  9. Serve immediately.
