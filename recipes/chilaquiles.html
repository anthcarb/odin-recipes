Gabriela Cámara's Chilaquiles

Fried eggs on chilaquiles verdes!

These chilaquiles are a perfect weekend breakfast.

Feel free to top with avocado and your choice of crumblly cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp plus 1 cup safflower oil
  • 1 qt salsa verde
  • 12 corn tortillas, cut into 8 wedges
  • 1/4 crème fraîche
  • 8 to 12 eggs
  • 1/2 cup white onion, finely minced
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1/2 feta cheese
  • 2 avocados cut into 8 slices

Steps

  1. Warm 1 tbsp oil over medium hear. Pour the salsa into the pan and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Warm 1 cup oil. Line a plate with paper towels.
  3. Fry tortillas wedges in batches for 2 to 3 minutes. Remove and place on paper towels.
  4. Fry eggs in oil for 1 min.
  5. Turn off heat of salsa pan. Add crème fraîche to salsa and mix.
  6. Drop chips into sauce and coat without breaking chips.
  7. Spread sauce and chips onto plate. Top with 2 fried eggs per plate.
  8. Top with onion, cilantro, feta, and avocado.
